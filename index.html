About the System

The Hospital Resource Management System facilitates efficient sharing of medical resources between healthcare facilities, ensuring critical supplies reach those who need them most. Our platform enables hospitals to register available resources and search for needed supplies from other facilities in their proximity.

During emergencies or shortages, quick access to medical resources can be life-saving. Our system helps bridge the gap between resource availability and demand, making healthcare more resilient.
